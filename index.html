About Us

Researching for nearly 6 months completing numerous trials, we have finally developed 25 dishes capturing the true taste of Amritsar.

To keep it authentic, we have sourced our ingredients from the heart of Amritsar and our recipes have been prepared with pure desi ghee. Combining the passion, experience and the relentless determination put in, we assure you the flavors will bring a smile on your face.
